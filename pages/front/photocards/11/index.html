About

Designing and creating artwork is a dream come true for me. To get where I am today, I graduated from Los Angeles Valley Community College, attended several illustration courses at the Art Center School of Design in Pasadena CA, and have worked both in-house and freelance for 23 years now.

Some of the terrific companies I’ve worked for are: Harry and David’s, The Time Factory, Quadriga Art, Jaftex Fabrics, Papel Giftware, Abbey Press, The Bacova Guild, Mosiac Design Works, Ganz, Barbour Publishing, Commonwealth Promotional Educational Supplies, Zody’s/Hartfield Advertising, French Graphics and a number of smaller businesses and individuals.

I now have artwork available for licensing suitable for all kinds of commodities. Please contact me or my agent, Alicia Dauber with Licensing Liaison for samples of available images. 360.224.5459 or email:alicia@licensingliaison.com . Many of my designs can now be sent via e-mail attachment as well as burned on a CD and sent traditionally.

I am an expert in CorelDraw 11, and use it as my vector program, which works well with Adobe Illustrator files. I am also proficient with both Photoshop 7 and Painter 9 and use it daily when working with four-color process images.

I am hearing-impaired, but not communication impaired. With the marvels of e-mail and the internet, I am able to be quick and effective for what ever project you need. My clients tell me that they like the way I get things done quickly and creatively with no hassles whatsoever. Fax is extremely helpful too 540.943.6457. My heart goes completely into everything I do, and I trust you will see that in my on going work. Thanks for stopping in.. and I hope to create something wonderful for your company too.
